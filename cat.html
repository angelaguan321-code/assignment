Fun Facts About Cats

  • Cats have a specialized collarbone that allows them to squeeze through tight spaces, similar to how they navigate narrow gaps.
  • A cat's purr vibrates at a frequency between 25 and 150 hertz, which may help with healing bones and tissues.
  • Cats spend about 70% of their lives sleeping or in a state of rest, meaning a nine-year-old cat has been awake for only about three years of its life.
  • Cats have a reflective layer behind their retinas called the tapetum lucidum, which enhances their night vision and makes their eyes glow in the dark.
  • A group of cats is called a "clowder," and a group of kittens is called a "kindle."
