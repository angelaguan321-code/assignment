Fun Facts About Dogs

  • Dogs have been domesticated for over 15,000 years.
  • There are over 340 different dog breeds worldwide.
  • Dogs have an extraordinary sense of smell, which is thousands of times more sensitive than humans.
  • Dogs can understand human emotions and often respond to their owners' feelings.
  • The Basenji dog is known as the "barkless dog" because it makes a unique yodel-like sound instead of barking.
