Fun Facts About Dogs

  1. Dogs have been domesticated for over 15,000 years.
  2. There are over 340 different dog breeds worldwide.
  3. Dogs have an extraordinary sense of smell, which is thousands of times more sensitive than humans.
  4. Dogs can understand human emotions and often respond to their owners' feelings.
  5. The Basenji dog is known as the "barkless dog" because it makes a unique yodel-like sound instead of barking.
